# Care Intelligence — Frequently Asked Questions

*AI products for healthcare centers*

---

**Q1: What is Care Intelligence?**
Care Intelligence is an AI company that builds intelligent software products for healthcare centers, including clinical documentation tools, patient triage assistants, and hospital operations analytics.

**Q2: What products does Care Intelligence offer?**
Our core products include CareScribe (AI medical documentation), CareTriage (symptom-checking and patient routing), CareInsights (operational analytics dashboard), and CareBot (patient FAQ and appointment assistant).

**Q3: Is Care Intelligence HIPAA compliant?**
Yes. All our products are built with HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, including encrypted data storage, audit logging, and role-based access controls.

**Q4: Who can use Care Intelligence products?**
Our products are designed for hospitals, clinics, diagnostic centers, and multi-specialty healthcare facilities of any size.

**Q5: How does CareScribe work?**
CareScribe listens to doctor-patient conversations (with consent) and automatically generates structured clinical notes, reducing documentation time by up to 60%.

**Q6: Does CareTriage replace doctors?**
No. CareTriage is a decision-support tool that helps prioritize patients based on symptom severity; final diagnosis and treatment decisions are always made by licensed clinicians.

**Q7: What languages does CareBot support?**
CareBot currently supports English, Hindi, Spanish, and French, with more languages planned.

**Q8: How is patient data stored and secured?**
Patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored in region-specific data centers, and access is restricted through multi-factor authentication and role-based permissions.

**Q9: Can Care Intelligence integrate with our existing EHR system?**
Yes, we offer integrations with major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, and Allscripts via HL7/FHIR standards.

**Q10: What is the pricing model?**
We offer subscription-based pricing tiered by facility size (small clinic, mid-size hospital, enterprise network), with custom enterprise plans available.

**Q11: Is there a free trial available?**
Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial for CareBot and CareInsights for new healthcare partners.

**Q12: How long does implementation take?**
Typical implementation takes 2–4 weeks, including EHR integration, staff training, and workflow customization.

**Q13: What kind of support does Care Intelligence provide?**
We offer 24/7 technical support, dedicated onboarding specialists, and quarterly performance reviews for enterprise clients.

**Q14: Does Care Intelligence offer analytics for hospital administrators?**
Yes, CareInsights provides real-time dashboards on patient flow, bed occupancy, staff utilization, and revenue cycle metrics.

**Q15: How can a healthcare center get started with Care Intelligence?**
Interested facilities can request a demo through our website, after which our team schedules a needs assessment and custom implementation plan.
